About Us

Why choose CWC Automotive?

ALWAYS A CAR IN OUR CARE

Located on Barge Way, between Iwade and the Eurolink, we are a garage offering MOT, Dealer Services, Diagnostics and Repairs to all makes of car and LCV.

If you struggle to find the time to get your vehicle to a garage we also offer a collection and delivery service. Combined with late opening during the week for appointments between 6pm and 8pm for those who work as hard as we do, it has never been easier to deal with maintenance of your vehicle.

Specialist dealer trained technicians in various manufacturers, with the latest diagnostic equipment gives you the confidence that your vehicle is in the very safest of hands.

We offer you a service that feels like your car has been into the main dealer, without the added cost.

Customer experience is a vital part of our service. We keep you informed of what is happening to your car and what is needed without confusing the situation. Our aim is to change how a garage operates for the better, and we believe that you will agree.

EuroRepar Car Service Partner

CWC Automotive is a proud member of the EuroRepar Car Service network — a Stellantis-backed multi-brand service and repair network with over 6,300 garages across 30 countries. This partnership gives us access to a massive range of quality parts, including OEM and leading aftermarket brands, while we continue to deliver the local, personal service you know us for.

Our Location

We are conveniently located in Sittingbourne, Kent, between Iwade and the Eurolink, serving customers throughout the local area and beyond. Our modern workshop is equipped with the latest tools and technology to handle all your vehicle needs.
